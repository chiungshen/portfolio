Background

In Taiwan, there is one bankruptcy fraud of the factory takes place per year in average. Because of the flaw in the present law, the employee victims could hardly receive any restitution, and still, there is no stiffer penalty for bankruptcy fraud. Anyone can be the next victim.

Client

Taiwan Employee Victim of Bankrupt Fraud Association(NPO)

Challenge

How to raise public awareness for bankruptcy fraud?

Idea

Salaryman Survival Kit

We built a survival kit for the office workers to be prepared to survive in unemployment. The survival kit contains 5 supplies and tools, each of them is no use in real life. We point out the following circumstance in an ironic way:

1. There is no proper recourse for bankruptcy fraud
2. Bankruptcy fraud could happen to any working class
3. Man-made disaster is more severe than natural disasters, everyone needs to prepare for the bankruptcy fraud.

01

Product Design

We believe people are more willing to pay attention to serious issues like bankruptcy fraud if they can digest the information with a touch of humor. Due to this consideration, we create a set of tools which is stated in the form of an ironic metaphor than of practical significance. Most of the tools in Salaryman Survival Kits are designed for people who lost their jobs to kill time in the parks. With this meaning, we hope people can realize that nothing can really help them go through bankruptcy fraud crisis, it will remain unchanged if they still choose to be onlookers instead of fighting for labor law revision and their deserved rights.

Collective Fund

Playflorm

02

Because of the limited media resource, just like promoting a latest unique product, we raise a donation at the crowd-funding platform to gain free exposure and to attract early adopters. We choose it as the key media dissemination channel since there are many public-spirited members caring about social issues. It was the first time crowd-funding platform was used not for raising money but for awareness.

03

Influencer Marketing

A mayoral election was held during the campaign, receiving a lot of attention. We took this opportunity and invited all these candidates to endorse Salaryman Survival Kits. Therefore, the great masses of people had the chance to learn more about the labor issues.
