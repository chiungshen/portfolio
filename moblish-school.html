Background

The usage of phones increased rapidly after the inventions of smartphones. It has been a world-wide discussed issue that smartphone addiction causes both physical and psychological harm. Yet, this issue is often discussed from intimidating and negative angle.

Challenge

How to talk about smartphone addiction with humor?

Idea

Mobilish School

Instead of telling people to put down their smartphones, we created a fake language to make people face smartphone addiction. The language is based on the insight that our talking behavior was changed by smartphones. Most of the time people just murmur like "Uh-huh" or "Hmm" as answers to others while they are busy checking their smartphones. It's just like they can communicate with these meaningless words and sounds, having their own language.

We exaggerated these words as the vowels of Mobilish to point out that though these words are meaningless, they still hurt. People should pay attention to their words and behaviors when they are on mobiles.

01

CI Design

In order to introduce to this fake language - Mobilish, which does not even exist in the real world, we designed the whole language system and a language school. In addition, we exerted world map in CIS and logo design to indicate that Mobilish is an international language spoken by smartphone addicts all over the world.

02

Longest Ad Ever

We shaped meaningless short sounds like hmm as the key factors of Mobilish. Furthermore, we released a 2-hour long commercial to promote it in a touch of irony. In the video, we created a scenario that a man spent 2 hours trying hard to explain a simple thing. When People feel bored and click the fake "Skip AD" button on the video, they would see how Mobilish is used to communicate in a more effective way. The video is more of a joke, but it reminds people of the truth that how ridiculous people talk with these meaningless sounds.
