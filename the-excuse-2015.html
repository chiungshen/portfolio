Background

Chinese New Year is coming. Heineken Taiwan wanted to encourage people to "Open Your World". How to develop thought-provoking ways to convey the brand message and coincide with the new year at the same time?

Client

Heineken Taiwan

Insight

People make goals and resolutions every New Year, but try to think back on the wishes we made over the past few years, most of them can hardly be remembered. Some people even made the same exact wish every single year, all because these New Year's resolutions have failed.

Challenge

Encourage people to stick to their New Year Resolutions.

Idea

Sky Lantern With Excuses

Many Taiwanese believe in releasing a lantern personalized with their wishes to start off the New Year on a right note. Instead of putting wishes on the lantern, this time, we wrote excuses that people usually make to cover up their failure, which represented the releasing of complaint and fears. Say goodbye to all these excuses and help make the resolution come true!

The video was implemented in two phases:
1.An anonymous fan page on Facebook to attract people to share their excuses.
2.Fly the sky lanterns with these excuses upon.

01

Anonymous FB Page

Around 400 excuses were submitted by anonymous users through a Facebook fan page. No one knew that these excuses would be adopted in the video until its release.

Renewed Tradition

02

The campaign was inspired by the insight into Taiwanese people's love of wish-making whether it's in temples, wishing upon a shooting star, while watching New Year's fireworks or by releasing sky lanterns. The campaign aimed to overturn the traditional culture of using lanterns for wishes and instead use lanterns for getting rid of excuses. It's a positive concept saying that before you make a wish, you should first say goodbye to your excuses.
