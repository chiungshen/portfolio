Background

The career training opportunity for SCI (Spinal Cord Injury) sufferers in Taiwan has been increasing in recent years, however, the job opportunity remains scarce for those received training. The reasons are tied to extreme lack of barrier-free environment in most enterprise facilities and the social stereotype towards "inability and low-productivity" for those SCI sufferers in wheels, which has made enterprises reluctant to recruit.

Client

New Life Information Services Co. Ltd. (NLIS)
NLIS is a social enterprise with accessible space, helping SCI sufferers to work on socially productive tasks.

Furthermore, for companies that do not hire sufficient qualified individual with disabilities to meet the regulation requirement, NLIS provides custom employment management services to co-locate these employees at NLIS disability friendly facilities to help companies to fulfill their legal duty, but also ease individuals with disabilities in access job opportunity.

NLIS does not accept financial donations, they aim to improve SCI sufferers' living quality and abilities for an independent life with self-confidence and dignity.

Challenge

How to raise public awareness for NLIS?

Idea

Deviate Office Parties

Is success directly proportional to the effort?

For many Taiwanese office workers suffering employment discrimination or unfair work environment, the answer is NO.

We released a series of funny ironic pictures about employment discrimination, linking these workers' experience with the discrimination of SCI sufferers have gone through, telling people "If you can endure your lazy boss, dysfunctional co-workers or even picky clients, why not accept a professional SCI sufferers as your partners?" Just like you and me, in many cases, SCI workers lack for nothing but an opportunity to prove themselves.
